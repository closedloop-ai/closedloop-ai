---
title: Team-Based Agentic Development
description: A model where teams, artifacts, and agents are coordinated together instead of optimizing only for individual output.
---

Team-based agentic development treats the team as the unit of optimization.

## Problem

Most AI workflows focus on helping one person produce output faster.

That is useful, but it does not solve:

- unclear ownership
- artifact drift
- broken review loops
- inconsistent execution environments

## What changes

ClosedLoop.ai uses artifacts, plans, loops, and judges to keep execution connected to team intent.

## Why it matters

The result is not just faster code generation. It is a tighter system for turning intent into shipped work.
