---
title: Control Plane
description: The place where work is defined, reviewed, coordinated, and governed across humans and agents.
---

The control plane is where coordination happens.

## Responsibilities

- define work
- structure artifacts
- launch execution
- review outputs
- preserve decision history

## Why ClosedLoop.ai centers this concept

Without a control plane, agent output floats free from the process that decides what should be built.

That increases local speed while increasing organizational ambiguity.
