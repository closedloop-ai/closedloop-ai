---
title: Approval
description: A human or rule-based decision that gates a specific gateway operation before it runs.
---

Approvals are governed by per-operation risk tiers (`low`, `medium`, `high`), a user-configured `defaultApprovalTier` (default `high`), and scoped `alwaysAllowRules[]` with default 7-day TTL. The cloud can force an approval with `x-desktop-force-approval: 1`.

Decisions resolve to `approved | denied | always_allow | expired`.

See [Approvals and sandbox (mechanisms)](../mechanisms/approvals-and-sandbox).
