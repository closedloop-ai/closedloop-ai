---
title: Context Pack
description: A bounded package of supporting information attached to a loop run.
---

A **context pack** is the material that subagents read during a loop: the primary artifact, investigation outputs, linked docs, injected organization learnings, and environment context.

Context packs are scoped to a budget (30K tokens for judge context) and assembled automatically by the `pre-explorer` subagent and the `artifact-type-tailored-context` skill.

See [Context packs](../mechanisms/context-packs) for details.
