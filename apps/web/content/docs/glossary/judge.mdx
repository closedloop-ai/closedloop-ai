---
title: Judge
description: An LLM-as-judge evaluator that scores an artifact or output against the artifact that launched the work.
---

Judges convert review from ad hoc opinion into structured, repeatable scoring. Every judge emits a `CaseScore` with per-metric thresholds and a `pass / fail / error` final status.

Closedloop.ai ships 21 judges (plan, code, PRD) in the `judges` plugin.

See [Judges (mechanisms)](../mechanisms/judges).
