---
title: PRD
description: A product requirements document that defines the problem, outcome, constraints, and success criteria for a piece of work.
---

A **PRD** is a product requirements document. In Closedloop it is a first-class artifact and the most common starting point for a loop.

A reviewable PRD has:

- a problem statement
- the desired outcome
- constraints
- measurable success criteria
- known risks

The four PRD judges (`prd-auditor`, `prd-dependency-judge`, `prd-scope-judge`, `prd-testability-judge`) score PRDs before planning.

See the [PRD template](../resources/prd-template) and [PRD to plan to execution](../workflows/planning-with-codex).
