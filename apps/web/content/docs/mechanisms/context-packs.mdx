---
title: Context Packs
description: Bundled context that gives loops the exact docs, artifact references, and constraints needed to execute well.
---

Context packs reduce ambiguity by packaging the right supporting material for a run.

## Good context packs are

- scoped
- artifact-linked
- explicit about constraints
- small enough to stay relevant

## Bad context packs are

- oversized
- stale
- disconnected from the task
